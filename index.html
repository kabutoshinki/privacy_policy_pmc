Privacy Policy

We are committed to protecting the privacy of our users and adhering to data security principles. This privacy policy outlines how we handle sensitive information related to devices and users in the prescription management application.

1. Information Collection

We collect information from users during the usage of the application. This information includes, but is not limited to:

  • Personal Information: Name, email address, phone number, and user account information.
  • Device Information: Device type, operating system, IP address, MAC address, and other technical specifications.

2. Use of Information

We use the collected information from users for the following purposes:

  • Service Provision and Improvement: Utilizing the information to provide, maintain, and enhance the quality of the prescription management service.
  • User Interaction: Sending notifications, updates, providing support, and responding to user inquiries.
  • Analysis and Research: Analyzing and researching data to improve user experience and the effectiveness of the application.

3. Information Security

We are committed to ensuring the security of user information and employ appropriate security measures to prevent unauthorized access, disclosure, or unauthorized alteration of personal information.

4. Information Sharing

We do not share, sell, trade, or rent users' personal information to third parties unless explicitly consented by the user or required by law.

5. Information Storage

We retain user information for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this Privacy Policy, unless a longer retention period is required or permitted by law.

6. User Privacy Rights

Users have the right to access, modify, or delete their personal information. Users also have the right to withdraw consent for the collection and use of personal information as stated in this Privacy Policy.

7. Changes to the Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time without prior notice. Any changes will be displayed within the application, and users should periodically review this policy.

If you have any questions or requests regarding this Privacy Policy, please contact us through the provided contact information within the application.
